Volunteer !

Are you interested in seeing what happens behind the scenes of preparing a big JSA event?

You are at the right place! Even if you aren’t on the E-board, you can still play an important role in our events!

Some of our events, mainly Harumatsuri (Japanese Spring Festival), run thanks to the gracious help of the many volunteers who not only help cooking but also help set up props to create an exciting atmosphere!

To volunteer at our events, please fill out the following form so that we can reach out to you!
