Past Harumatsuri

2018


March 25th 2018

1:00PM - 4:00PM

@ Sherman Function Hall

Our theme for 2018 was "Sei-shun" (blue spring), which refers to youth, adolescence, and the complex emotions everyone experiences during this time.

For lunch, we will be serving homemade bento boxes and oden! There will be many performances by special guests, as well as a kimono corner where you get to take pictures in kimonos!




2017



April 22nd 2017

12:00PM - 3:00PM

@ Sherman Function Hall

Our theme for 2017, Blossom, not only represented spring, flowers, and joy, but also the ‘blossoming’ of our club as a whole.

The festival featured HERMIT, TRADITIONAL MUSIC CLUB, and off campus professional guests, MICHIKO KURATA (Japanese folk dancer) and MICHIKO IMAI (Japanese calligrapher)!






2016

@ ICC

We brought back the Yukata wearing, green tea ice cream, and spicy ramen challenge from the previous year!

The event featured Karate club, Anime club, and Language Table!
